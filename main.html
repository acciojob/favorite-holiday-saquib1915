Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station in Himachal Pradesh, India. It is famous for its snow-covered mountains, scenic valleys, adventure sports, and peaceful environment, making it one of the most popular holiday destinations.

  1. Solang Valley - Best place for adventure sports.
  2. Rohtang Pass - Famous for breathtaking snow views.
  3. Hadimba Temple - A historic and peaceful temple.
  4. Old Manali - Known for its cafes and local culture.
  5. Beas River - Perfect for rafting and sightseeing.
  • Siddu - A traditional Himachali dish.
  • Babru - A delicious stuffed bread.
  • Trout Fish - A popular local specialty.
  • Chha Gosht - A flavorful mutton curry.
  • Mittha - A sweet Himachali dessert.
